What we do

Prepare for adventure

From the waters of Port Phillip Bay to the mountains of Victoria, we do it all.

⛵

Sailing & Sea Skills

Learn to sail on Port Phillip Bay. Sailing courses, power boating, kayaking and water activities — it's in our DNA as Sea Scouts.
⛺

Camping & Outdoors

Overnight camps, bushwalking, abseiling, rock climbing and navigation. We get outdoors every term, rain or shine.
🧗

Action & Adventure

Mountain bike riding, caving, water skiing, rafting, skiing at Falls Creek — plus community service and leadership challenges.
🪸

Reef Environment Project

Our Venturer Unit monitors Port Phillip Bay's reef ecosystem at Holloway Bend — real science, real impact, Commonwealth funded.

Annual highlights

Something to look forward to

Every year we run major events on top of the regular weekly program.

Feb
🌊

Surf Camp — Anglesea

Annual surf camp on the Great Ocean Road — open to Scouts and older.

May
🏕️

Survival Camp

Annual highlight for Scouts and Venturers — a multi-day bush survival challenge.

Jun
🍳

Master Chef Night

Cubs compete in our popular annual cooking challenge — the results are always delicious!

Sep
🛒

District Billy Cart Derby

Cubs build and race billy carts against other Packs in the Bayside District — engineering meets fun.

Nov
🏕️

Gilweroo — Scouts Camp

Annual regional Scout camp held in November — patrol cooking, challenges and patrol spirit.

Jul
❄️

Falls Creek Ski Trip

Rover Crew heads to Falls Creek for a week of skiing at our annual ski chalet trip.
