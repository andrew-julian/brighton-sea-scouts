FAQ

Common Questions

How do I enrol my child? +

Contact our Group Leader Janet Cardell by phone or email, or submit the enquiry form above. She'll arrange for your child to attend three free trial nights. If you'd like to proceed after the trial, enrolment is done online through Scouts Victoria's MyScout portal.

What age can my child start? +

Children can join Joey Scouts from age 5. The full programme runs through to age 26 across five sections: Joeys (5–7), Cubs (7–10), Scouts (10–14), Venturers (14–17), and Rovers (17–26).

Are there waiting lists? +

Waiting lists can apply, particularly for younger sections. Contact us to check current availability. We recommend enquiring early — Brighton is a popular group.

What's the cost? +

Scouts Victoria membership is approximately $555 per year (billed twice annually). This covers insurance, badges, programme materials, and most weekly meeting activities. Camps and major activities have additional costs. Financial assistance is available — please ask Janet.

How do I become a leader or volunteer? +

We always welcome enthusiastic adults who want to make a difference. No prior Scouting experience is needed. Contact Janet and she'll walk you through our straightforward onboarding process, which includes a Working with Children Check and a Scouts Victoria induction.

Where are you located and how do I get there? +

We're at 34 Wilson Street, Brighton VIC 3186. The hall is a short walk from North Brighton station (Sandringham line) and Bay Street. There is off-street parking on Wilson Street and surrounding streets.
